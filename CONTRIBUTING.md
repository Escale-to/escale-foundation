## What We Welcome

We welcome contributions that:

- Reinforce the core concepts of time, travel, and intention
- Improve clarity, calmness, or coherence
- Simplify without reducing meaning
- Enhance accessibility without introducing urgency
- Respect privacy, restraint, and transparency
- Align with the project‚Äôs long-term vision

Examples include:

- Conceptual clarifications
- Documentation improvements
- Design or UX refinements aligned with the philosophy
- Thoughtful technical proposals that preserve intent

---

## What We Do Not Accept

We do not accept contributions that:

- Introduce instant delivery or bypass waiting
- Optimize for speed, engagement, or retention metrics
- Add notification pressure or urgency
- Turn Escale into a social network or productivity tool
- Prioritize novelty over coherence
- Add features that cannot be expressed using Escale‚Äôs core language

Technical sophistication alone is not sufficient.  
Alignment with intention is mandatory.

---

## Tone and Collaboration

Escale values calm, respectful, and thoughtful collaboration.

- No urgency
- No pressure
- No performative debate
- No growth-at-all-costs mindset

Disagreement is acceptable.  
Disruption of intention is not.

---

## Decision Process

Final decisions aim to preserve:

- conceptual clarity,
- ethical restraint,
- and long-term coherence.

Not every good idea belongs in Escale.

---

## A Shared Responsibility

Contributing to Escale means participating in a shared responsibility:
to protect a slower, more intentional form of digital communication.

If this resonates with you, you are welcome here.
